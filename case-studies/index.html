Windows & Doors · Trade
Illustrative

Cutting the quoting process from half a day to under 20 minutes

Every quote required manually pulling supplier prices, calculating margins in a spreadsheet, and typing up a PDF to email out. Each quote took 2–3 hours and the system relied entirely on one person who knew how it all worked.

75% Time saved per quote
20 min Average quote time

A structured quoting tool was built that pulled pricing automatically, applied the correct margins, and generated a branded PDF in one click. Anyone in the business could now produce a quote.

Custom web app Google Sheets API PDF generation Email integration
Service Business · 6-Person Team
Illustrative

Leads going cold for 3 days. Response time dropped to under 10 minutes.

Enquiries came in through a contact form, landed in a shared inbox, and were replied to whenever someone got around to it — sometimes hours later, sometimes the next day. High-intent leads were going cold before anyone called back.

<10 min Response time
0 Leads uncontacted

The form was integrated with a CRM. An automated acknowledgment was sent immediately. The right person was notified via WhatsApp within seconds. No enquiry could be missed.

Admin-Heavy Business · 8 Staff
Illustrative

Three disconnected tools. One integrated system. 4 hours of admin saved weekly.

Three tools in active use — a CRM, a job management platform, and a separate invoicing system — with no connection between them. Staff were manually copying client details between all three for every new job. Time-consuming, error-prone, and frustrating.

~4 hrs Admin saved weekly
0 Manual data transfers

Integrations were built between all three platforms. New client data entered once automatically populated the job tracker and invoicing system. Completion triggers invoices. No manual steps in the middle.

Make Pipedrive Tradify Xero
Sales Team · 5 People
Illustrative

Pipeline tracked in a spreadsheet no one trusted. Replaced with a working system.

A 5-person sales team tracking their pipeline in a shared spreadsheet with no consistent format, outdated rows, and no clear view of what was actually open, dead, or won. Management couldn't trust the numbers. The team hated updating it.

Full Pipeline visibility
1 view Single source of truth

A proper CRM was set up, configured for their specific sales process, and connected to their email. The team was onboarded practically — not trained in a classroom. Within a week, it was in active daily use.

CRM configuration Email integration Pipeline setup Team onboarding
Founder-Led Business
Illustrative

Weekly reporting that took 2 hours, automated to run itself every Monday morning

Every Monday, the founder spent 90 minutes to 2 hours pulling data from three sources, formatting it into a report, and sending it to two team members and a silent investor. It was the same process, every week, without fail — and eating a Monday morning every time.

2 hrs Recovered every Monday
7am Report delivered before work starts

An automated pipeline was built to pull data from all three sources, assemble the report in the same format used before, and email it to the correct recipients — every Monday at 7am, without anyone doing anything.
