Company Requirements

Companies must have:

  1. A minimum of one director. One must be EEA resident or else an insurance bond is required. We can arrange.
  2. One secretary. If one director only, this person cannot be secretary as well. We could provide a secretary.
  3. A minimum of one shareholder.
  4. A registered office, a home address would suffice.
  5. PPS Number required for directors. If no PPS number, must register for an IPN Number. We can arrange.
  6. PPS Numbers and eircode’s are now required by CRO for all directors and an IPN Number for those who don’t have a PPS Number including persons resident abroad.
